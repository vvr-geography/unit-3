How Forcasted Heat Risk Impacts Incarcerated Communities

Incarcerated communities are on the frontlines of climate change. Many prisons, jails, and detention facilities lack the appropriate mechanisms to keep incarcerated folks safe and comfortable during extreme heat events. Moreover, most also lack emergency preparedness plans for climate related emergencies and climate adaptation plans for the increasingly devastating forcasted climate impacts.
